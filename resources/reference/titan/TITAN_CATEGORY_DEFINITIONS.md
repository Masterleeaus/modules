# Titan BOS — Category Definition Strategy
## From Feature Surface → Operational Control Plane

---

## THE STRATEGIC SHIFT

**ZeroPay** is category-defining because it **owns the payment completion moment** — not as a payment processor, but as a **revenue control plane**.

To make the entire Titan BOS suite equally category-defining, **each app must own a critical operational moment** and become the control plane for that moment.

---

## THE NINE OPERATIONAL MOMENTS

### 1. **Titan Hello** — Owns Service Entry
**What It Does**: Not a booking widget. A business entry intelligence system.

**The Moment**: Customer inquiry → system qualifying/scoping/staging before technician knows the job exists.

**Control Plane Functions**:
- **Intent-to-Job Engine**: Detects quote, urgent, inspection, renewal, complaint → routes to correct workflow
- **Multi-Channel Memory**: SMS → email → portal → voice — conversation continues seamlessly
- **Smart Scope Builder**: Builds property profile, complexity flags, access constraints before scheduling
- **Booking Confidence Scoring**: "92% bond clean with agent inspection risk" → auto-triggers checklist preset
- **Pre-Dispatch Readiness**: Confirms parking, pets, electricity, handover expectations before job exists

**Business Impact**: Fewer field surprises. Better data quality. 15–20% higher rebooking.

---

### 2. **Ground Zero** — Owns Execution Timing
**What It Does**: Not a scheduler. A state-transition orchestrator.

**The Moment**: Job scheduled → job dispatched → job executed → next job optimized.

**Control Plane Functions**:
- **Lifecycle Graph Scheduling**: Schedules states (rough clean → progress clean → handover clean), not time slots
- **Constraint-Aware Dispatch**: Optimizes travel time + certification + equipment + weather + SLA + deadline
- **Zone-Based Mapping**: For construction verticals, dispatch happens spatially (zones → stages → completion)
- **Delay Cascade Simulation**: If job A shifts 45min, predicts impact on 7 downstream jobs + suggests fixes
- **Dispatch Risk Meter**: Warns "handover clean at risk" before failure occurs

**Business Impact**: More jobs per technician. Fewer domino failures. Higher SLA compliance.

---

### 3. **Titan Go** — Owns Work Correctness
**What It Does**: Not a field checklist app. An execution quality control system.

**The Moment**: Technician starts job → executes with confidence → job is inspection-ready.

**Control Plane Functions**:
- **Context-Aware Mutation**: Checklist adapts live (site type, weather, inspection risk, client tier, asset condition)
- **Evidence Confidence Engine**: Photo insufficient? Requests retake automatically. Inspection-ready guaranteed.
- **Skill Matching Layer**: Hides tasks beyond technician certification. Locks biohazard steps without training.
- **Offline-First Chain**: Photos, signatures, scans, notes captured with tamper-evident sequencing
- **Completion Confidence**: Predicts inspection pass probability before job closes

**Business Impact**: 2–3 extra hours cleaning per technician. Zero comeback disputes.

---

### 4. **Titan Pro** — Owns Decision Visibility
**What It Does**: Not a dashboard. A decision-trigger engine.

**The Moment**: Manager sees current operations → recognizes pattern → acts before problem occurs.

**Control Plane Functions**:
- **Intervention Radar**: Alerts on failures likely, late payments predicted, overload risk, SLA breach probability
- **Margin Heatmap**: Shows which services profitable, which loss-making (e.g., bond cleans ✓, windows ✗)
- **Compliance Exposure**: Current certification gaps, missing docs, audit risk areas — all visible
- **Client Risk Forecast**: Churn likelihood, complaint probability, late payment tendency
- **Workforce Stability**: Burnout patterns detected early

**Business Impact**: Data-driven strategy. Spot problems before they cost money.

---

### 5. **Titan Zero** — Owns Institutional Intelligence
**What It Does**: Not an AI assistant. A cognitive operating system.

**The Moment**: Decision needed → AI provides institutional context → decision is smarter.

**Control Plane Functions**:
- **Procedural Memory**: Remembers how *this company* performs jobs, not just documentation
- **Inspection Strategy Advisor**: "Agent checks window tracks in this suburb" → adjusts checklist
- **Training Gap Detector**: Identifies skill gaps preemptively, schedules learning
- **Artefact Auto-Composer**: Photos + checklist + timestamps + notes → bond pack/report instantly
- **Policy Translator**: ISO guideline → executable checklist logic (bridges docs and execution)

**Business Impact**: Faster onboarding. Fewer compliance mistakes. Institutional knowledge doesn't walk out door.

---

### 6. **Zero Fuss** — Owns Trust Continuity
**What It Does**: Not a messaging tool. A relationship continuity system.

**The Moment**: Job starts → property manager informed → job completes → proof delivered → trust maintained.

**Control Plane Functions**:
- **Expectation Alignment**: Before job, confirms arrival window, outcome definition, access rules
- **Readiness Confirmation**: "Property staged successfully" — proactive transparency
- **Evidence Streams**: Live job timeline instead of file attachments — builds confidence
- **Satisfaction Prediction**: Detects dissatisfaction before complaint arrives
- **Silent Approval**: Learns clients who accept without replying — reduces unnecessary follow-ups

**Business Impact**: More PM referrals. Smoother renewals. Fewer disputes.

---

### 7. **Titan Studio** — Owns Demand Creation
**What It Does**: Not a training toolkit. A service growth accelerator.

**The Moment**: Operator runs business → growth opportunities surface → campaigns trigger automatically.

**Control Plane Functions**:
- **Service Gap Detection**: "Local suburb lacks bond cleaners" → suggests campaign
- **Seasonality Engine**: Predicts solar surge months, pool windows → auto-triggers campaigns
- **Reputation Flywheel**: Requests reviews at optimal emotional timing (post-success evidence delivery)
- **Upsell Detection**: Client booked window clean → suggests gutter clean campaign automatically
- **Local Authority Positioning**: Builds "inspection-ready specialist" brand per vertical

**Business Impact**: Built-in growth engine. Predictable demand.

---

### 8. **Titan Solo** — Owns Operator Stability
**What It Does**: Not a lightweight mode. An operator's brain-load reducer.

**The Moment**: Operator wakes up → day is planned → jobs execute → income is stable.

**Control Plane Functions**:
- **Daily Route Optimizer**: Auto-plans travel, job order, breaks, supply stops
- **Income Stability Predictor**: Forecasts weekly variance, suggests fill-in jobs
- **Smart Repeat Booking**: Detects renewal-due clients, auto-schedules outreach
- **One-Tap Accounting**: Finish job → invoice + receipt + payment + follow-up instant
- **Expansion Advisor**: "Time to hire first staff member" — rare but valuable

**Business Impact**: Less admin. More focus on quality. Visible growth path.

---

### 9. **ZeroPay** — Owns Revenue Completion
**What It Does**: Not a payment processor. A revenue control plane.

**The Moment**: Job complete → customer ready to pay → payment instant → cash in hand.

**Control Plane Functions**:
- **Payment Sessions**: Secure, PCI-compliant, device-first
- **QR Payments**: Customer scans, pays — no card reader needed
- **Payment Links**: Text link, customer pays from home
- **Alternative Methods**: PayID, bank transfer (no card required)
- **Zero Fees**: Margin protection for operators

**Business Impact**: Faster collection. Less cash handling. Better cash flow.

## WHY THIS FRAMEWORK WORKS

Each control plane:
1. **Owns a critical decision point** in the operator's day
2. **Reduces cognitive load** at that moment (operator doesn't have to think, system thinks)
3. **Automates away duplication** (enters data once, system propagates)
4. **Surfaces intelligence** (shows what's about to happen, not what happened)
5. **Triggers action** (alerts before problems, suggests opportunities before they're obvious)

Together, they form a **true service-business operating system** where:
- Every operational moment is owned by a specialized control plane
- All control planes read/write the same operational record
- No data duplication across apps
- No learning curve friction (vertical terminology baked in)
- No platform dependency (operator controls the business, not the other way around)
