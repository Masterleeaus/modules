- *Pressure Washing*: Damage reports with photographic evidence, surface assessment summaries

### Compliance Automation — Stop Managing Spreadsheets

Cleaning operators spend 3–4 hours per week managing compliance paperwork (SWMS, JSA, PPE checklists, certifications, audit trails). Most use email + spreadsheets.

**Titan automates it.**

- **SWMS & JSA Auto-Generation**: Site-specific hazard assessments and job safety analyses generate from job context (location, weather, equipment, team experience level)
- **PPE Verification Tracking**: QR-based punch-in confirms PPE worn before technician starts job
- **Certification Tracking**: System tracks expiring certifications (first aid, RSA, confined space, etc.) and alerts before they lapse
- **Chain-of-Custody Workflows**: Every job step tracked: who did what, when, where, and what evidence was collected
- **Audit Logs**: Compliance inspector demands proof of safe work? You have timestamped, photographic evidence of every step

### Evidence-First Execution — The Job *Proves* Itself

Most cleaning operators rely on customer trust and memory. Disputes happen. Invoices get contested. Damage claims stall.

**Titan bakes evidence into the job itself.**

- **Before/After Photos**: Automatically linked to job ID, timestamped, geotagged, and ready for customer handoff
- **QR Presence Verification**: Technician scans QR code on arrival. Proves who was where and when.
- **Checklist Scoring**: Inspectors score each step (0–100). System flags substandard work in real-time.
- **Damage Reporting**: Technician finds existing damage? They document it with photo + description before work begins. Zero liability.
- **Client-Ready Documentation**: Job complete? System auto-generates a handover report with photos, checklist results, and service summary. Clients see proof, not just an invoice.

**Outcome**: Fewer disputes. Faster invoicing. Happy customers who can *see* what they paid for.

### **TIER 1: FIELD EXECUTION**

#### **Titan Go** — Field Execution Operating System
*Owns the critical operational moment: **work correctness***

**Category**: Guided Work Engine — Not just a checklist app. An execution quality control system.

**What It Does** (the control plane):

- **Context-Aware Checklist Mutation**: Checklist adapts live based on site type, weather, inspection risk, client tier, and asset condition. Execution becomes dynamic, not rigid.
- **Evidence Confidence Engine**: Photos insufficient for bond-grade verification? System requests retake automatically. Inspection-ready output guaranteed.

### **TIER 2: OPERATIONS CONTROL**

#### **Ground Zero** — Lifecycle Orchestration Engine
*Owns the critical operational moment: **execution timing***

**Category**: Stage-Aware Dispatch System — Not just a scheduler. A state-transition orchestrator.

**What It Does** (the control plane):

- **Lifecycle Graph Scheduling**: Instead of calendar slots, schedule job states. Example: rough clean → progress clean → handover clean. System predicts next slot automatically.
- **Constraint-Aware Dispatch AI**: Optimizes based on travel time, technician certification, equipment availability, weather, handover deadline, and client SLA. Not just availability—intelligence.
- **Zone-Based Work Mapping**: For construction/multi-stage verticals, site becomes zones → stages → completion heatmap. Dispatch happens spatially, not temporally.
- **Delay Cascade Simulation**: If job A shifts 45 minutes, system predicts impact on 7 downstream jobs and suggests fixes before failure occurs.
- **Dispatch Risk Meter**: Warns "handover clean at risk" before failure occurs. Enables early intervention.

**Why It Matters**: Dispatchers make smarter decisions because they see state dependencies, not just time slots. Work flows in the right sequence at the right moment.

**Outcome**: Faster scheduling. More jobs per technician. Fewer domino failures. Higher SLA compliance.
